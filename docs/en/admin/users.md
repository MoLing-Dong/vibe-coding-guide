# Human and AI Roles

AI can speed up coding, but it should not replace your product judgment, architecture decisions, or final acceptance. Stable vibe coding comes from clear roles.

## You Own

- Goals and priorities.
- Whether a solution fits the business.
- Real context and constraints.
- Review of critical code and security risks.
- The final decision to merge, release, or roll back.

## AI Owns

- Searching and summarizing code structure.
- Proposing implementation options and trade-offs.
- Writing repetitive or local code.
- Adding tests, docs, and checklists.
- Debugging errors and explaining failures.

## Collaboration Rhythm

1. You provide the task and boundaries.
2. AI gives its understanding and plan.
3. You confirm direction.
4. AI implements in small steps.
5. AI verifies and reports.
6. You review the final result.

## Do Not Fully Delegate

- Real money, permissions, or security-sensitive changes.
- Data migrations without rollback plans.
- Product-heavy interaction redesigns.
- Architecture changes without team agreement.

AI is an execution and reasoning amplifier, not a way to transfer responsibility.
